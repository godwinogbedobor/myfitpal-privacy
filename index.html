1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information.

a) Account information

  • Email address and password, if you sign up using email. These are stored and authenticated securely through Firebase Authentication. Your password is never visible to us in plain text. It is handled entirely by Firebase's authentication service.
  • Google account information, such as your name, email address, and profile identifier, if you choose to sign in with Google.
  • Username that you choose within the App.

b) Health and fitness information you provide

  • Body measurements including weight, height, age, and gender. These are used only to calculate your personalized calorie and macronutrient targets.
  • Your fitness goal, such as weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain.
  • The weight history you choose to log over time.
  • Food and meal entries you log, including food names, quantities, calories, macronutrients such as protein, carbohydrates, and fat, and the time each entry was logged.
A note on the word "biometric": MyFitPal's food tracking calculations rely on body measurements such as weight, height, age, and gender, which are sometimes described internally as "biometrics" in the fitness sense of the word. This is entirely different from biometric authentication data, such as a fingerprint or facial scan. MyFitPal does not access, collect, or store fingerprint data, facial recognition data, or any other biometric identifier from your device.

c) Photos

If you use the camera scanning feature, we temporarily process the photo you take of your food. Please see Section 2 below for a full explanation of how this works.

d) Subscription and purchase information

  • Your subscription plan and premium status, which are managed through Google Play Billing and RevenueCat.
  • MyFitPal never receives or stores your full payment card number, bank details, or security code. These are handled entirely by Google Play's payment system.

e) Information collected automatically

  • Basic app usage information stored locally on your device, such as cached meal logs, your theme preference, and your streak count, so that the App continues to work smoothly offline.
MyFitPal does not use third party advertising software, analytics or tracking software, or data broker integrations of any kind. We do not display advertisements within the App.

2. Food Photos and AI Analysis

When you scan a meal using your camera, the following takes place.

  1. The photo is sent securely, encrypted in transit using HTTPS, from your device to MyFitPal's backend server.
  2. The backend forwards the image to Google's Gemini AI service for analysis, which identifies the food and estimates its calories and macronutrients.
  3. The image is processed in memory only. It is not saved to our servers, our database, or any other form of persistent storage. It exists only for the brief moment required to generate your nutrition estimate, and is discarded immediately afterward.
  4. Only the resulting text data, such as the food name, estimated calories, and macronutrient breakdown, is saved to your account. The photo itself is never retained.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To calculate your personalized calorie and macronutrient targets.
  • To allow you to log, view, and track your meals and weight history over time.
  • To analyze food photos and generate meal ideas using artificial intelligence.

  • To maintain the security and integrity of the App, for example by preventing duplicate usernames and detecting fraudulent subscription activity.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your food logs or body measurement data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • Your account data and meal logs are stored in Firestore, a cloud database service operated by Google, and are protected by security rules that restrict access to your own data only.
  • Some data is also cached locally on your device, in local storage, so that the App continues to run smoothly while offline.
  • Your camera log and manual log history is retained for thirty days, after which older entries are automatically removed from the log history view.
  • We retain your account information for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide you with the App's services.
  • If you delete your account, as described in Section 9, we delete your associated personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where certain records must be retained for legal or accounting purposes.

7. Security

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers, including Firebase, our backend API, and the Gemini API, is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and TLS.
  • Access to your data in our database is restricted by security rules that allow only you, as the authenticated account owner, to read or write your own data.
  • We do not store your full payment details. These are handled directly by Google Play Billing.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to do the following.

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information. Most profile fields can be edited directly within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent, where our processing is based on consent, for example by logging out and discontinuing use of the App.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 13.

9. How to Delete Your Account and Data

You may request deletion of your MyFitPal account and all associated personal data, including your profile information, body measurements, meal logs, weight history, and subscription linkage, at any time by following these steps.

  1. Send an email to 9to5gymbro@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request," sent from the email address associated with your account.
  2. We will verify your request and permanently delete your account data within thirty days.

Please note that deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. To stop being charged, please cancel your subscription separately through the Google Play Store app, under Menu, then Subscriptions.
